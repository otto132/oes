# Eco-Insight Revenue OS — Internal Beta Quick Start

Welcome to beta testing! Here's everything you need to get started.

## Login

Sign in with your Google account at the app URL. You should already have access.

## What to Test

### Core Workflows (start here)

1. **Dashboard** — Your home screen. Shows pipeline stats, next-best-actions, today's meetings, and at-risk deals.

2. **Approval Queue** — The heart of the AI system. AI agents suggest outreach emails, lead qualifications, account enrichments, and tasks. Review the reasoning, then approve, edit & approve, or reject each suggestion.

3. **Pipeline** — Your deals board. On desktop, drag deals between stages (forward only). Click any deal to see health scores, contacts, and activity history.

4. **Leads** — New prospects in a kanban view. Advance leads through stages (New → Researching → Qualified), then convert qualified leads into accounts and deals.

5. **Accounts** — Your customer/prospect database. Click an account to see contacts, opportunities, activity timeline, and tasks.

### Secondary Features

6. **Inbox** — Synced emails classified by AI (positive reply, objection, question, etc.). Use keyboard shortcuts: `j`/`k` to navigate, `e` to archive, `t` to create a task.

7. **Tasks** — Action items with assignees, due dates, subtasks, and comments with @mentions.

8. **Meetings** — Calendar view of upcoming meetings. Use the Prep tab to prepare and the Outcome tab to log results.

9. **Signals** — Market intelligence detected by the Signal Hunter agent. Convert interesting signals into leads.

10. **Weekly Digest** — AI-generated pipeline summary.

## Tips

- **Search:** Press `Cmd+K` (Mac) or `Ctrl+K` (Windows) to search across accounts, deals, leads, and contacts.
- **Mobile:** The app works on phones. Pipeline and Import are best on desktop. Everything else works on both.
- **Theme:** Toggle dark/light mode from the sidebar (bottom).

## Known Limitations

- **Import:** Keep CSV files under 500 rows.
- **Pipeline kanban** is desktop-only. Mobile shows a card list.
- **Digest page** sidebar layout is optimized for desktop; mobile shows a stacked view.
- Keyboard shortcuts (Cmd+K, j/k navigation) are desktop-only.

## How to Report Issues

Reply to the beta thread with:
1. **What you did** (steps to reproduce)
2. **What you expected** to happen
3. **What actually happened**
4. **Device/browser** (e.g., Chrome on MacBook, Safari on iPhone)
5. **Screenshot** if possible
